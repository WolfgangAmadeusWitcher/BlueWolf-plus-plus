# Code of Conduct

BW++ is an open and respectful community. We do not tolerate harassment or discrimination.

## Our standards
- Be kind and constructive.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Focus on the work and keep feedback professional.

## Enforcement
Report issues to the maintainers (contact details TBD). Maintainership will review and respond.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces and public interactions related to BW++.
